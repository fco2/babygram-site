Delete Your BabyGram Account

You can request deletion of your BabyGram account and all associated data at any time.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (instant)

  1. Open BabyGram
  2. Tap your profile icon (top right)
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the prompt

Your account and all baby data are permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request via email

If you no longer have the app, email us at babygram.app@gmail.com with the subject BabyGram Account Deletion Request.

Include the email address associated with your account. We will delete your account and data within 30 days.

What data is deleted?

  • Your account credentials
  • All baby profiles
  • All activity logs (feeding, sleep, diapers, etc.)
  • Subscription and settings data
